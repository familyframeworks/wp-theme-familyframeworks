Amy Pakhomov

Amy Pakhomov has 18 years of experience as an occupational therapist and holds a master’s degree in education. She will work to alleviate the strong reactions to sensory stimuli experienced by some children. Amy will incorporate realistic ideas that work within the family’s schedule, improving the child’s ability to cope with real-life sensory input.
